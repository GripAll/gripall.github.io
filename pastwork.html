Drop&Pickup and Inventory,Hotbar slot system

This contains 3 systems in 1, Theres a keybind supported weapon slot system which also supports dragging Also makes it able to equip armor pieces on body. You are able to drop and pick up dropped objects freely. You are also able to drop mutiple items in a bag and pick up what you need. You are able to store and drop various materials in the inventory next to these.

R6 TPS Gun System

An R6 third party gun system, bullets using a physics module, the shell also falls on the ground when a shot is fired. You are able to reload at marked stations, fully editable. Uses material based bullet smoke particles. Each limb on characters has health, The limbs explode when their health is exactly 0.

Air Defense

Air defense system for background decoration and immerse in the scene.

The shots coming from the sky actually breaking the terrain down upon hit, and kills players if they get hit by it.

The air defense turret's task is to try to prevent the projectiles landing on the ground.
However only 1 turret can work at the same time, they switch duties over time.
